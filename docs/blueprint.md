# **App Name**: SpinTech Steel

## Core Features:

- Home Page with Interactive Effects: Dynamic landing page showcasing the company's expertise and key offerings with modern visual effects.
- About Us Section: Detailed company history, mission, and values with interactive timelines and employee spotlights.
- Areas of Expertise: Interactive map and descriptions highlighting key regions and industries served, integrated with client testimonials.
- Metal Alloys Catalog: Comprehensive catalog of metal alloys with detailed specifications, interactive 3D models, and AI-powered search functionality to find the right alloy based on specific parameters.
- Qualification & Certifications: Showcase of company qualifications, certifications, and industry standards compliance using animated infographics.
- Business Deals Overview: Dedicated section highlighting successful projects, partnerships, and case studies using visually engaging formats.
- Contact Form: Fully responsive contact form with interactive submission confirmations and integrated location map.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, steely blue (#3B5998) to represent strength, reliability, and the industrial nature of the company.
- Background color: Light gray (#E9E9EB) for a clean, modern look that contrasts well with the primary color.
- Accent color: A vibrant, contrasting orange (#FF8C00) to highlight call-to-action buttons and interactive elements, adding energy and modernity.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif for headlines to create a tech-forward impression. Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif to give a clean, modern, and readable appearance.
- Use modern, minimalist icons that reflect the industry and technological focus.
- Employ a grid-based layout with plenty of white space for a clean and structured design. Incorporate parallax scrolling effects to add depth and visual interest.
- Use subtle animations and transitions throughout the site to enhance the user experience, such as fade-in effects, slow-motion video backgrounds, and interactive hover effects on key elements.
